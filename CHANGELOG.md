# Changelog

All notable changes to the Western PA Fly Fishing Dashboard will be documented here.

---

## v1.1.0

### Improvements
- Updated river gauges to preferred fishing locations:
  - Clarion River — Cooksburg
  - Oil Creek — Rouseville
  - French Creek — Meadville
  - Redbank Creek — Brookville
  - Allegheny River — Franklin
- Added gauge location identification.
- Improved confidence in river condition recommendations.
- Enhanced wade safety evaluation.
